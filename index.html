I'M NOT DYING TODAY.

I am not dying from ALS. I will die from something else. Dying is not an option.

“

When they told me I had ALS, they said I had 2 to 5 years. That was over 6 years ago.

I'm not a doctor. I'm a husband, a father, and a bodybuilder who got told he was going to die. After my diagnosis, I spent months buried in research, reading clinical studies, talking to other patients, and slowly building a supplement protocol that I felt good about. It practically became an obsession.

Today I'm still walking and still talking, and I've lost friends to this disease along the way. That's the reason I'm sharing all of this. I don't want anyone to have to piece together what took me thousands of hours to figure out on my own.

Whether you're a patient or a caregiver staying up late researching for someone you love, I built this site for you.
