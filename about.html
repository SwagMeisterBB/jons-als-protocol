I'm Jon Edy

Husband. Father. Fighter. 6+ years with ALS and still walking.

The Diagnosis

When I received my ALS diagnosis, the doctors told me what they tell most patients: you have 2 to 5 years. The median survival for ALS is brutal. There are only a handful of FDA-approved treatments, none of which are cures.

I could have accepted that timeline. Instead, I did what I've always done — I fought back.

Building the Protocol

I started researching. I read clinical studies, talked to other ALS patients, consulted with specialists, and began building my own supplement protocol — one supplement at a time, one study at a time.

I discovered L-Serine, an amino acid that showed promise in a Phase 1 clinical trial for ALS patients. I added HMB for muscle preservation. I built a daily routine around timing, dosing, and consistency.

Over the years, my protocol grew to roughly 20 supplements, organized into three categories: vitamins, bodybuilding supplements for muscle support, and what I call "the potent stuff" — the supplements backed by the most compelling research for ALS.

Still Walking

Six years later, I'm still here. I'm still walking. I'm still talking. I still have the energy to fight.

I'm not a doctor. I'm not a researcher. I'm a patient who did his homework and built a system that has worked for me. Every person's ALS journey is different, and I make no promises about what my protocol can do for anyone else.

But I believe every ALS patient deserves access to the same information I have — the exact supplements, the exact brands, the exact doses, and the mindset that keeps me going.

Why I Share This

For years, I've been sharing my supplement list informally — over phone calls, in support groups, at doctor's visits. Other ALS patients ask me what I take, and I tell them. Many of them start taking the same supplements.

This site exists to make that information available to every ALS patient who wants it, not just the ones who happen to know me personally.
